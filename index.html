When someone is homelessness, means they've got kicked out of their previous home and living on the streets with no money, food, and water. Poverty means people with not much money will not be able to afford food, education, housing, childcare, clothing etc, to take care of their needs.



World populations of homeless people (all ages)

  • The world population of homeless people (including children) under the age of 18 is 18.3%.
  • Another world population of young adults ages 18-24 is 7.8%.
  • Lastly, the world population of adults age 24 and older is 73.9%.

World Populations of homeless people (all races)

  • The world population of whites are 48.3%.
  • Another world population of Black/African Americans are 39.4%.
  • Another world population of Asians are 1.3%
  • Next, The population of Native Americans are 3.3%.
  • Lastly, the population for multiple races are 6.1%.

World Populations of homeless people (all genders)

  • The world population for females are 38.5%.
  • Next, the world population for males are 60.7%.
  • Lastly, the world population of Transgenders are 0.5%.
